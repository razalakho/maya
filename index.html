Who We Are

We built Roam because
travel shouldn't be this hard.

Roam was founded on one belief — the best experiences in life happen when you leave your comfort zone. We exist to make that first step feel a little less scary and a lot more affordable for every Gen Z traveller in the UK.

🎯
Our Mission
Make personalised travel planning accessible, affordable, and anxiety-free for every young person — regardless of budget or experience.
💡
Our Approach
AI-first. Budget-first. Human always. Maya gives you honest advice like a well-travelled friend, not a commission-hungry agent.
🌱
Our Values
Transparency, accessibility, and genuine care for every traveller — whether it's their first solo trip or their fiftieth adventure.
🚀
Our Vision
A world where no young person stays home because travel felt too confusing, too expensive, or too scary to attempt alone.
